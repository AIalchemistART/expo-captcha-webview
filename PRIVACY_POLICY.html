Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Mystical Bible Companion ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app and related services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Supabase.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized data about your use of the app (e.g., selected passages, features used) to improve our services.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device information for analytics and troubleshooting.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app’s core features.
  • To improve user experience and app functionality.
  • To communicate updates, respond to inquiries, and provide support.
  • For security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share data with trusted third-party services (e.g., Supabase, CAPTCHA providers) solely for app functionality and security.
  • We comply with legal requests where required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections and secure authentication. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices

  • You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time from within the app.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related requests or questions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and/or on our website. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us
